So, who am I?

Hello, my name is Michał, and I'm from Poland. At the moment of creating this landing page I'm 26 years old. I have no background in computer science but that doesn't stop me from learning programming.

I was always known for my interest in computers but being honest I was more into gaming than into computers from their technical side. When I was really young, my aunt's friend taught me a few things in C++ but quickly dropped the idea of being my mentor as he had other stuff to take care of in his life. My interest in coding slowly perished only to be rekindled recently (beggining of 2023).
